## 2026 Programs

The purpose of the Asian Chamber of Commerce is to ensure our Asian owned businesses, both small and large, are equipped to take on the challenges of growing beyond these challenging times. With multiple webinars, community virtual
                      gatherings and our annual business conference, we create opportunities to foster new relationships and provide economic trade both locally and internationally. Please take time to participate and support us in 2026 and beyond.

Each year, The Asian Chamber of Commerce in Houston recognizes companies and individuals who have impacted our community
                      at large. The event unites the Asian business community to celebrate excellence in leadership, business, and community impact within the greater Houston area.

The Asian Chamber will be hosting our annual Trade Forum where companies and individuals can learn about import/export trade and the opportunities/risks involved in moving to a global market, especially in Asia.

May is Asian American and Pacific Islander Heritage Month and The Asian Chamber is helping the Houston culinary sector celebrate by promoting Asian inspired dishes and raise awareness of Asian owned restaurants throughout the city.

The Asian Chamber of Commerce wants to help your business win bids and learn how buyers look for suppliers. Be coached by decision makers from mayor corporations and the public sector in researching bids, learning the application process,
                      and how to knock out a winning presentation.

For the 8th year, Asian Chamber will host an executive learning environment for participants to complete a 8-week intensive fast track program taught in English as well as Mandarin. Each student will be immerse with learning the essential
                      business principles of growing your organization.

The Rising 10 Awards highlight the Top 10 fastest growing Asian-owned companies in the greater Houston area. This spotlights successful companies with 3+ years in operation. The Asian Chamber of Commerce will recognize the Rising 10
                      recipients at our Business Conference luncheon.

Each quarter we host our luncheons, networking nights and company tours to gives members opportunities to network and learn from topical issues related to business development, management and outside factors that have impact to profitability
                      and sustainability.

We host regular learning sessions to help educate our members on growing their operations and gaining useful tools to stay competitive. We invite speakers to share resources on business topics related to Asia and the Asian American
                      community.
