# Membership Info

The Chamber offers multiple membership levels. Our doors are open to people and businesses of diverse backgrounds, not strictly the Asian community. You will find high-quality speakers and programs, and stay informed on a variety of news and events as a Chamber member.

A few universal benefits include discounted rates to luncheons and events, listing in the member directory, annual listing in the Chamber e-newsletter and access to special news and resources distributed to members only.

## Membership Details

Membership is 365 days from when you join. Renewal letters will be sent out a month from when it expires. Beginning January 2026, we will go to an annual membership starting from January to December and depending on when you join, you will be prorated.

We want you to get involved and help support the Asian Chamber of Commerce. Please email info@asianchamber-hou.org or call us at 713-782-7222 to explore opportunities to sponsor.

The Asian Chamber of Commerce is a 501c6 nonprofit. Membership with the chamber is a business expense and not tax deductible. Charitable giving through the Asian Chamber Foundation (501c3) will be considered as tax deductible.
