# Problem Statement

## What problem are we solving?

ACC (Asian Community Center) is overwhelmed by repetitive customer inquiries about:
- Membership fees
- Benefits and programs
- How to join
- General organizational information

These questions require manual responses from staff, consuming significant time that could be spent on strategic activities and community building.

## Who are the users?

**Primary users:**
- Prospective members researching ACC
- Current members with questions
- Community members seeking information

**Secondary users:**
- ACC administrative staff (who will save time)

## Why is this important?

As ACC grows, inquiry volume increases proportionally. The current manual process:
- Creates administrative overload
- Leads to slower response times (delays can frustrate potential members)
- Reduces staff capacity for high-value work
- Doesn't scale effectively

Many questions are repetitive and could be automated with consistent, accurate answers available 24/7.

## What happens today without this solution?

**Current state:**
- Staff manually responds to every email/call/message
- Response time varies (hours to days)
- Same questions answered repeatedly
- No 24/7 availability
- Staff burnout from repetitive work

**Impact:**
- Potential members may lose interest during wait times
- Staff have less time for strategic planning and community engagement
- Inconsistent responses depending on who answers

A chatbot can handle the majority of routine questions instantly, freeing staff to focus on complex issues and relationship building.
